Has vaccination improved public health in England? - hypothesis testing

As preliminary analysis of vaccine effectiveness, hypothesis testing can be used to identify significant differences in daily cases or death counts. To begin testing whether COVID-19 vaccinations have been effective in England, the data will be split into two time periods. This split will be made on {{ data["dateOfHalfVacc"] }}, when half the population became fully vaccinated. This date is chosen because even today, not everyone is vaccinated, so a reasonable time should be chosen where enough people have been vaccinated to show if there is an effect or not.
